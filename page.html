Eagle hunters

Pictures from a vanishing world

Mongolia's far west Ethnic Kazakhs number around 100,000 and are the largest minority in Mongolia. They are mostly settled around the nation's desolate far west.

Life as a nomad During the long winters, the eagle hunters leave their homes and head into the mountains on horseback.

A hunter's bond "I took a picture of an eagle on its back, it's upside down like a baby."

Free again After several years of hunting, eagles are released back in to the wild to live out their final years in freedom.
